№ 2 Background

Before moving to Denmark in 2010, Georgian has lived in Suceava, Romania, where he began his journey. He received his Bachelor’s degree in Horsens, Denmark. Georgian studied ICT Engineering, an interdisciplinary degree with the focus on the development and management of information systems.

After receiving the Bachelor’s degree, he shorty began his Master’s degree in Aarhus, Denmark. He studied Computer Engineering, a degree with a curriculum with a focus in software engineering.

In 2011, he began his freelance career working on small design and prototyping projects. Later on, he began working on web and software development projects.

At the beginning of 2013, Georgian began his career at Level11, a small Horsens-based start-up, developing software using ASP.NET, PHP and the Umbraco CMS.

In September of 2013, Georgian left Level11 to start working for Aarhus University, where he helped designing and developing an educational platform, scienceathome.org, as a Student Full-Stack Developer & UX Designer. He worked there with PHP, Twitter Bootstrap, HTML5/CSS3 and MySQL. He stayed with AU until his Bachelor’s graduation in February 2014.

Since June 2014, Georgian worked for Cetrea, a Danish healthcare start-up, as a Software Developer & UX Designer. He there wrote modular components using JavaScript, AngularJS, CoffeeScript, Less, HTML5/CSS3, jQuery, among others. He also helped wire-framing, designing high-fidelity prototypes and writing design solution descriptions for the Front-End department.

In August 2016, Georgian left Aarhus for London, starting a job as a Lead Software Engineer for Future First, working with OO JavaScript, Meteor, AngularJS, MongoDb, Angular Material, Less, HTML5/CSS3, Underscore, among others. He also architects and manages internal projects while line managing one in-house developer and outsourced staff.
